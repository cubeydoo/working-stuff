Lines of Action

Lines of Action (LOA) is a board game invented by Claude Soucie.

Rules

Each player takes turns moving one of their pieces in any direction, A player wins when all of their own pieces are adjacent to one another, whether diagonal or in a cardinal direction.

Controlling Play

A players piece may not hop over an opposing players piece, unless it is landing on said piece in which case it is destroyed. Each player can only move a piece as many squares as there is pieces in that direction.
